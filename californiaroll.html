California Roll

Description

Inexpensive fan favorite sushi roll style!

Ingredients

  • Crab meat
  • Medium Cucumber
  • Toasted Nori Seaweed
  • Sesame Seeds
  • Sushi rolling mat

Steps

  1. Wash the rice with cold water, drain and follow rice maker instructions
  2. Wrap sushi roller in plastic wrap!
  3. Fold pieces of nori in half to split them
  4. Spread a generous handful of sushi rice onto nori
  5. Flip nori over and place fillings across the center lengthwise
  6. Start rolling matt firmly keep the roll in place
  7. Cut evenly
