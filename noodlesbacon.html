Noodle's Bacon and Cottage Cheese

Description

This irresistible noodle dish is a popular recipe in the Hungarian cuisine!

Ingredients

  • Egg noodles
  • Cured bacon
  • Cottage cheese and sour cream

Steps

  1. Slice bacon into bite size pieces
  2. Cook bacon until crispy and set aside
  3. While bacon is cooking boil the noodles
  4. Strain noodles and transfer back into pot
  5. Add in bacon and mix in cottage cheese and sour cream
  6. Serve while hot!
