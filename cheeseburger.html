Simple Cheese Burger Recipe

Description

Follow this simple recipe to have a tasty Cheese Burger!

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Seasoning blend
  • Sliced Cheese
  • Toppings (Tomato, Onions, Ketchup, etc.)
  • Buns

Steps

  1. Preheat grill to medium high to high heat
  2. Prepare the patties to desired size mixing in seasoning
  3. Grill the Hamburgers
  4. Add on sliced cheese
  5. Remove from grill and serve on bun
